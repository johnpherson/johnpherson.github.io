Hacker News AI Posts

I've been noticing a lot of AI posts on Hacker News as of late and I was curious to see if the number of posts would increase or decrease a year from now. This is by no means scientific, just a fun project to keep track of the AI topic on HN. This DAG runs once a day and the data is automatically updated.

  • Uses Airflow for orchestration.
  • Airflow DAG kicks off every day with cron (this is running on my home machine).
  • Stores data in PostgreSQL.
  • Sends Slack notifications upon completion.
  • Visualizes data with D3.js.
