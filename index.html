Hacker News AI Posts

This is an Airflow project the pulls down the top ten most popular posts from Hacker News and analyses how many of them had the A.I. keyord in them on that day. This prject used Airflow for task orchestration and stored the data in postgres. I used a webhook to notify a slack channel when the data pipeline has finished and visualized the data in D3
