Hacker News AI Posts

This is an Airflow project that pulls down the top ten most popular posts from Hacker News and analyses how many of them had the A.I. keyword in them on that day. This project uses Airflow for task orchestration and stores the data in PostgreSQL. I used a webhook to notify a Slack channel when the data pipeline has finished and visualized the data in D3.
