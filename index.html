Hacker News AI Posts

I've been noticing a lot of AI posts on Hacker News as of late and I was curious to see if what kind of an increase I would be seeing over time. This is by no means scientific, just a fun porject to kaap track of the AI topic in the top ten most popular posts on HN. This DAG runs once a day and the data is automatically updated.

  • Used Airflow for orchestration.
  • Stored data in PostgreSQL.
  • Sent Slack notifications upon completion.
  • Visualized data with D3.js.
Airflow DAG Data Visualization

Airflow DAG

This DAG hits the Hacker News endpoint to bring down the top ten most popular posts of the day.

  • Ingests data from the HN API.
  • Inserts data directly into postgres staging table.
  • Error handeling.

Slack Notification

Once the pipeline is completed, a message is posted to a Slack channel to inform me of the completed DAG.

  • Custom completion message.
  • Uses a webhook to integrate with Slack.
  • Just plain fun.
